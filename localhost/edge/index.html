    New Jerseyans have always looked out for one another, and we're leading a movement of students throughout New Jersey to ensure that none of our friends, our family, or our neighbors are left without health insurance simply because they were unsure of how to access it.

    Far too often, people find that they lack health insurance
    just when they need it.

    Whether a young adult or a new American, living without health insurance means that every day is a gamble. Catastrophic medical expenses and bankruptcy are always just an injury or accident away.

    Most uninsured individuals know this. They know how precarious it is to be uninsured and want health insurance. They are, however, simply overwhelmed by the health care marketplace. What they need are helping hands and one-on-one conversations to help them make a well-informed decision about their health insurance options.

    Over 250,000 New Jerseyans are uninsured.

    The majority of them want insurance, but over 50% feel overwhelmed by the process.

    Knowing that low-cost plans, financial support, and in-person help are available can mean the difference between enrolling and not enrolling.

    That's where Be Jersey Strong comes in.

    In September 2015, we will train and mobilize 1,000 students to have over 100,000 conversations with New Jerseyans about their health insurance options.

    Be Jersey Strong will work with regional coordinators to identify and train campus captains and volunteers at every two- and four-year college in New Jersey.

    Campus captains and volunteers will then speak at churches, clinics, and community centers to connect individuals to the information and resources they need to make a well-informed decision about their health insurance options.

    Together, we will make New Jersey strong.
